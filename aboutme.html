Biography

My name is Ashley Lindsey, and I am a student at the University of Washington iSchool pursuing my MS in Information Management, specializing in User Experience, and hopefully Information Architecture as well.

I am new to the field, so I'm interested in learning as much as I can. I am particularly interested in accesibility design and the ways technology can be imagined to help marginalized populations in society.

I currently live in Seattle, WA with my boyfriend and a cat named Mara.
