Hi! I'm Ashley.

I’m currently pursuing a Master of Science in Information Management from the University of Washington’s Information School, specializing in user experience design and information architecture. I also received my BA from the University of Washington, majoring in History, Political Science and a minor in Labor Studies.

This site is primarily a place for me to share my work and portfolio as I progress through my studies. It also will serve as a coding playground, as I practice my CSS/HTML and eventually JavaScript skills.

I grew up on Bainbridge Island, WA, just a short ferry ride from downtown Seattle. I moved to Seattle proper in 2009 when I started at UW the first time, and I‘ve been here ever since.

When I am not working on school stuff (which is increasingly rare these days) I enjoy knitting and crafting all sorts of things, baking desserts, and running so I can enjoy said desserts.
